# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of Coffee_shop
Queried restaurant transaction data to surface top-performing menu items and patterns in how customers spend.
Assessed pricing structures and category-level sales to generate actionable business insights.
Pinpointed the 5 highest-spending orders and broke down their composition to profile high-value customer behavior.
Surfaced trends in order frequency and high-volume purchases to flag opportunities for operational improvements.
